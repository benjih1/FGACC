About

Cycling seems to go hand-in-hand with the world of anaesthesia – from the crowds of lycra clad staff in the mornings, to the overheard conversations about weekend rides amongst the coffee room chatter.

Many of us ride together, but I hope to make it easier for us to organise group rides.

The club is in its very early stages. As it grows, we will arrange rides and update them on this website. Rides will be grouped by level, and eventually we will organise women-only rides.

It is just me running it currently, but again, as the club grows, I will be looking for help – so let me know if you fancy helping out.

I am talking with a local cycle clothing company about designs for jerseys if people are interested in those.
