About

I am setting this club up as a way for anaesthesia and critical care staff across Yorkshire to organise group rides. There are so many people across our specialties who cycle, so I would love bring us all together, after a rough start to 2020.
The club is for anyone who loves cycling, regardless of ability.
If you are interested in joining, email me or drop me an message using the form below, and follow us on Twitter.
